# City Traffic Ordinances
        
        ## Speed Limits
        - Downtown: 25 mph
        - Residential: 20 mph
        - Highways: 55 mph
        
        ## Parking Regulations
        - No parking in bike lanes
        - 2-hour limit in commercial zones
        - Permit required in residential zones
        
        ## Traffic Control
        - Obey all traffic signals
        - Yield to pedestrians
        - No U-turns in business districts
